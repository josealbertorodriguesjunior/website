Our philosophy ...

The timeless wisdom of old holds answers to many questions humanity faces today. These powerful concepts draw from a deep and realistic understanding of nature and man kind's position in it.

The Subutai Social Foundation looks to this wisdom to inspire greatness and ingenuity in meritocratic open technology collaboration.

Meritocracy
It's important to let experience and knowledge drive the decision making process in communities. With the proper analytics tools, project member rankings help drive the meritocratic governance model so do'ers may lead.
Flagship
product
Subutai Social is the flagship product of the SSF: we derived the foundation's name from it. Peer-to-Peer cloud and fog computing frees the cloud from the confines of the data center. This true social cloud allows everyone to share their Internet connection and compute resources safely with others to build their own cloud environments. The cloud flows freely, moving and changing shape, to adapt to load. Incidentally the SSF infrastructure runs on Subutai Social.
